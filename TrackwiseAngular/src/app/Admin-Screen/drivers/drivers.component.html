Welcome to LTT Help Centre!


- To search for an driver, enter your search query in the search bar then proceed to click on the “Search” button.
- To add a driver, click on the “Add Driver” button that will navigate you to the add driver screen.
- To edit a driver, click on the “Edit” button beside the driver you want to edit. The system will navigate you to the edit driver screen.
- To remove a driver, click on the remove button then confirm the removal.


If you can’t add, edit or remove a driver, please follow these steps: If the driver can’t be removed, please do the following:
  1. Ensure that you clicked the “Yes” button on the confirmation modal.
  2. Make sure that you are connected to the internet.
  3. Log out of your account and then proceed to log back in afterwards.

If the issue still persists, please contact our support team at hanru@limtt.co.za.
