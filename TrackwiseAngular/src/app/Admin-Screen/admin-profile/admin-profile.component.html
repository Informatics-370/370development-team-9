Welcome to LTT Help Centre!


To edit your profile:
  1. To edit your profile, click on the “Edit Profile” button on the bottom of the screen.
  2. A form will display where you can update your profile details.
  3. To save the changes you made, click on the “Save Changes” button.
If your changes did not save, please do the following:
  1. Ensure that you clicked the “Save Changes” button before closing the page.
  2. Make sure that the Name, Surname and Password fields are not left empty.
  3. Make sure that you are connected to the internet.
  4. Log out of your account and then proceed to log back in afterwards.
