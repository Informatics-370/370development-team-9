Welcome to LTT Help Centre!


- To search for a supplier, enter your search query in the search bar then proceed to click on the “Search” button.
- To add a supplier, click on the “Add Supplier” button that will navigate you to the add supplier screen.
- To edit a supplier, click on the “Edit” button beside the supplier you want to edit. The system will navigate you to the edit supplier screen.
- To remove an supplier, click on the remove button then confirm the removal.


If you can’t add, edit or remove a supplier, please follow these steps:
  1. Refresh the webpage.
  2. Log out of the system and then log back into the system.
If the supplier can’t be removed, please do the following:
  1. Ensure that you clicked the “Yes” button on the confirmation modal.
  2. Make sure that you are connected to the internet.
  3. Log out of your account and then proceed to log back in afterwards.

If the issue still persists, please contact our support team at hanru@limtt.co.za.
