Welcome to LTT Help Centre!


- To log into your account, enter your email address and password in the correct fields.
- Then click on the “Log In” button.


*--Please note that you need to be registered before you can log in--*

- If you are not yet registered, click on the “Register here” link at the bottom.
- If you forgot your password, click on the “Forgot password” link at the bottom.


If you can’t log in, please try the following:
  1. Ensure that your details are correctly entered.
  2. Make sure that you are connected to the internet.

If the issue still persists, please contact our support team at hanru@limtt.co.za.
